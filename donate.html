Making a Donation

If you would like to make a cash donation, please click here to visit the event registration page and use the third option which is a donation only. Your donation will go towards raffle prizes or helping us to cover the cost of running the event. Thank you in advance for your support.

Raffle Donations

One of the best ways to support this event is by donating something for our raffle. The raffle helps us draw in big crowds and collect more toys for the Marines. We are a registered non profit organization and because of that your donations are tax deductible. If you or your business would like to make a donation please download our donation request letter below for more information.

Attending the Party

If you are attending the party, you can either bring a toy or a donation. If you bring a toy, we ask that you bring them unwrapped. Please make sure the toy is not a weapon or any type of projectile. If you would like to give a cash donation, please make your check payable to "CLE Cares, Inc."

Volunteers

We are looking for anyone who would like to help out with the event. If you are interested in joining our team please contact us.
