About Toys for Shots

With hopes of providing a larger donation, a Chinese Auction will be the center attraction for this year's fund raiser. To give this year's event the best opportunity for success, we are inviting a donation from the local pro-athletic organizations, restaurants, and entertainment businesses. Each donation will be organized in a "themed" style basket to generate the highest level of interest and raise as many bids as possible. Your participation will be greatly appreciated and rewarded by having your organization's name posted at the event and on the event website, if requested. Please help us make this Christmas happy for the communities' children.
